# Privacy Policy for WhereIsIt Inventory

**Last Updated: December 1, 2025**

## Introduction

WhereIsIt Inventory ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address and password when you create an account
- **Inventory Data**: Item names, descriptions, quantities, prices, locations, and categories you enter
- **Photos**: Images you take or upload of your inventory items
- **Shopping Lists**: Items you add to your shopping lists
- **Container Information**: Container names and organization data

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App features used, crash reports, and performance data
- **Camera Access**: Only when you actively use the photo or barcode scanning features
- **Local Storage**: Data stored on your device for offline functionality

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the app's inventory management functionality
- Sync your inventory data across your devices
- Enable sharing with household members (when you choose to do so)
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Send you notifications about low stock items (if enabled)
- Provide customer support
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance features

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Most of your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite
- Photos are stored locally on your device
- Data persists even when offline

### Cloud Sync (Optional)
- When you enable sync, encrypted data is transmitted to our secure servers
- We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data transmission
- Server data is encrypted at rest
- We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes

### Security Measures
- Password-protected accounts with secure authentication
- Encrypted data transmission
- Regular security audits
- Access controls to prevent unauthorized access

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

- **With Your Consent**: When you invite household members to share your inventory
- **Service Providers**: Third-party services that help us operate the app (e.g., cloud storage, analytics)
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition (users will be notified)

## Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services:
- **Google Vision API**: For image recognition in shelf scanning (images processed temporarily, not stored)
- **Analytics Services**: To understand app usage (anonymized data)
- **Cloud Storage**: For optional backup and sync features

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and all associated data
- **Export**: Download your inventory data in CSV/JSON format
- **Opt-Out**: Disable sync and keep all data local-only
- **Control Sharing**: Manage who has access to your shared inventories

### How to Exercise Your Rights
- Delete data: Use in-app settings or contact us
- Export data: Use the Export feature in app settings
- Opt-out of sync: Disable sync in account settings
- Delete account: Contact support@whereisit-inventory.com

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Camera and Photo Permissions

- **Camera**: Used only when you take photos of items or scan barcodes
- **Photo Library**: Used only when you select existing photos
- **Permissions**: You can revoke these at any time in device settings
- **Storage**: Photos stored locally; optionally synced if you enable cloud backup

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data may be deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of deletion request
- **Backup Copies**: May persist in backups for up to 90 days

## International Data Transfers

If you access our app from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located in the United States or other countries. By using the app, you consent to such transfers.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending an in-app notification
- Email notification for significant changes

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
