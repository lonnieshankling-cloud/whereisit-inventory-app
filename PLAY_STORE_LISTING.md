## Store Listing Tips

1. **Test your descriptions:** Have someone unfamiliar with the app read them
2. **Use keywords naturally:** Don't keyword stuff
3. **Highlight unique features:** Focus on what makes your app special
4. **Update regularly:** Keep descriptions fresh with new features
5. **Localize later:** Start with English, add languages as you grow
6. **Screenshots tell the story:** Make them clear and compelling
7. **Respond to reviews:** Shows you care about users
